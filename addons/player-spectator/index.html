Player Spectator

With the Player Spectator add-on, you can now spectate other players in real-time, just like in other popular games! This add-on lets you follow any player on your server, observing their actions and movements without interfering in their gameplay. Unlike Minecraft’s traditional spectator mode, which allows you to explore the world independently, this add-on is focused on spectating specific players directly. Perfect for server admins, content creators, or friends who want to watch each other’s gameplay up close, the Player Spectator add-on brings a new level of engagement and control to multiplayer Minecraft!

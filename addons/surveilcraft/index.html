SurveilCraft: CCTV

SurveilCraft is a unique CCTV add-on for Minecraft Bedrock Edition, designed to bring advanced surveillance features right into your game. With SurveilCraft, players can place their own CCTV cameras, setting them up at any distance and still accessing the live feed. Each player has individual control over their own cameras, ensuring privacy, while the cameras remain invisible to players for enhanced stealth. Perfect for creating hidden surveillance systems, managing large builds, or adding a new level of security and immersion to multiplayer gameplay.

Custom Cameras

Custom Cameras, a powerful and highly customizable add-on for Minecraft Bedrock Edition. Whether you're crafting cinematic scenes, building security systems, or just exploring your world from unique angles, this addon gives you full control over the camera like never before.

Create dynamic tracking cams, static cinematic shots, or anything in between. Every camera is fully customizable—position, rotation, type, behavior—and all data is saved permanently. No command block chaos, no limitations—just pure visual freedom.

The best way to understand the power of this addon? Yeah by trying it out!!
